LOCAL LABEL CONFERENCE 2017 - BOSTON ™

The Local Label Conference 2017 - Boston ™ is a meeting for all to come learn more about the local alternative music scene and to learn more about the labels that help create the scene.

The conference is being held at the Hynes Convention Center in Boston, MA from October 20-22.
